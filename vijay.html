Vijay

Vijay, whose full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is an Indian actor, playback singer, and politician who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. He was born on 22 June 1974 in Madras, Tamil Nadu, India. Vijay is referred to by fans and media as "Thalapathy" (commander) and is the highest paid actor in Tamil cinema.
